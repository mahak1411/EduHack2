Photosynthesis Overview

Key Process

Photosynthesis is the biological process by which plants, algae, and some bacteria convert light energy (usually from sunlight) into chemical energy stored in glucose molecules.

Chemical Equation

6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light energy → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Main Components

  • Chloroplasts: Organelles where photosynthesis occurs
  • Chlorophyll: Green pigment that captures light energy
  • Stomata: Pores that allow gas exchange
